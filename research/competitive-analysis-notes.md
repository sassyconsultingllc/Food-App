## Competitor 1: Restaurant Roulette!! (Turbolaser LLC)

**Platform:** iOS (iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Vision)
**Rating:** 4.5 stars (1.6K ratings)
**Price:** Free with in-app purchases ($0.99 to remove ads)
**Size:** 9.6 MB
**Last Updated:** April 2024

### Features:
- Random restaurant picker with spin wheel
- Location-based search (GPS, map, or zip code)
- Filter by food category, price, distance
- Food categories: Asian, BBQ, burgers, cajun, Chinese, crepes, dessert, Hawaiian, Indian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, kosher, Mexican, pizza, sushi, Thai, vegan, vegetarian, waffles
- View restaurant ratings, food types, distance, price
- Links to Yelp pages
- Get directions to chosen restaurant
- Add your own restaurants manually

### User Complaints (from reviews):
- Doesn't find chain restaurants well
- Finds too many gelato/cookie places
- Some trendy bars not found even if on Yelp
- Users want more filters (delivery, outdoor seating, groups, kids)
- Users want Yelp collections import

### What Foodie Finder Does Better:
- Sentiment analysis with 400+ phrases
- Culver's Flavor of the Day integration
- Multi-source rating aggregation (not just Yelp)
- Daily specials display
- Menu links and ordering options (DoorDash, UberEats, etc.)
- Recently viewed history
- Dark mode toggle
- Dietary restriction filters (9 options)
- Open Now filter with real-time hours checking
- Share functionality
- Photo carousel
- Price range display on cards

### What Restaurant Roulette Has That We Don't:
- Established user base (1.6K ratings)
- Apple Vision support
- User can add custom restaurants manually
- Yelp web page integration

## Competitor 3: Yelp (The Giant)

**Platform:** iOS, Android, Web
**Funding:** Public company (NYSE: YELP), ~$2.7B market cap
**Monthly Users:** 178 million+

**Key Issues Users Hate About Yelp:**
- 1,847 BBB complaints in last 3 years
- Accused of "pay to play" - filtering positive reviews for non-advertisers
- Filters out authentic positive reviews
- Permits illegitimate negative reviews
- Small businesses universally hate it
- Predatory business practices alleged

**Yelp's New AI Features (2024):**
- AI-powered Review Insights
- Yelp Assistant (conversational AI)
- Enhanced business discovery

**Opportunity:** Users are actively seeking Yelp alternatives due to trust issues

## Crowdfunding Platform Comparison

| Platform | Best For | All-or-Nothing | Fees |
|----------|----------|----------------|------|
| Kickstarter | Creative/Tech projects | Yes | 5% + 3-5% processing |
| GoFundMe | Personal causes | No | 5% + 3% processing |
| Indiegogo | Diverse campaigns | Optional | 5% + 3% + 30¢ |

**Kickstarter Stats:**
- 65% of projects get fully funded
- Higher pledges than all other platforms combined
- Best for tech/creative projects

**GoFundMe:**
- No existing community (relies on your network)
- Keep whatever you raise
- Better for personal causes

**Recommendation for Foodie Finder:**
Kickstarter is better fit because:
1. Tech/app category
2. Higher average pledges
3. Built-in community of backers looking for new projects
4. All-or-nothing creates urgency
