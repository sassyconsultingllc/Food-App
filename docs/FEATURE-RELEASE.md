## 🎉 Introducing Foodie Finder

**Can't decide where to eat? Let fate decide.**

Foodie Finder is the fun, intelligent way to discover your next meal. Spin the wheel, explore nearby restaurants, and make dining decisions effortless.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎰 Random Restaurant Picker
- **Spin Wheel** - Fun, animated wheel with haptic feedback
- **Quick Spin Widget** - One-tap randomization from the home screen
- **Group Decision Mode** - Perfect for settling debates with friends
- **Spin History** - Track your past spins and discoveries

### 📍 Smart Location
- **GPS Auto-Detection** - Finds restaurants near you automatically
- **Custom Zip Code** - Search any area, anywhere
- **Adjustable Radius** - 1 to 25+ miles

### 🍽️ Powerful Filters

**18+ Cuisine Types**
American, Italian, Mexican, Chinese, Japanese, Thai, Indian, Mediterranean, Vietnamese, Korean, Greek, French, BBQ, Seafood, Pizza, Burgers, Sushi, and more.

**9 Dietary Restrictions**
- Vegetarian
- Vegan
- Gluten-Free
- Halal
- Kosher
- Dairy-Free
- Nut-Free
- Keto
- Low-Carb

**Additional Filters**
- Open Now
- Price Range ($, $$, $$$, $$$$)
- Minimum Rating

### 🧠 Intelligent Insights

**AI-Powered Sentiment Analysis**
Our proprietary system analyzes reviews using 400+ restaurant-specific phrases to give you honest assessments:
- ✅ "Highly Recommended"
- ⚠️ "Mixed Reviews"
- ❌ "Proceed with Caution"

**Multi-Source Rating Aggregation**
We don't rely on a single source. Foodie Finder combines ratings from Google, Foursquare, and HERE to provide trustworthy, unbiased scores.

### 🍦 Culver's Flavor of the Day
A Midwest exclusive! See today's frozen custard flavor at nearby Culver's locations. Plan your dessert destiny.

### 📱 Restaurant Details
- **Photo Carousel** - Browse restaurant images
- **Menu Links** - View menus before you go
- **Hours & Contact** - Know when they're open
- **Directions** - One-tap navigation
- **Order Options** - Links to DoorDash, UberEats, Grubhub, and more

### 🗒️ Personal Features
- **Favorites** - Save restaurants you love
- **Personal Notes** - Add private notes to any restaurant
- **Recently Viewed** - Quick access to past discoveries
- **Spin History** - Remember your wheel results

### 🎨 Experience
- **Dark Mode** - Easy on the eyes
- **Celebration Effects** - Confetti when you find a winner
- **Sound & Haptics** - Tactile feedback (with mute option)
- **Share** - Send restaurant picks to friends
