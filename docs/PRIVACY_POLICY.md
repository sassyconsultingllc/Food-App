## Introduction

Sassy Consulting ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Foodie Finder mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Preferences:** Default zip code, search radius, and theme settings you configure in the App.
- **Favorites:** Restaurants you mark as favorites.
- **License Information:** If you activate a license, we collect your email address and device identifier for license validation purposes only.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Location Data:** With your permission, we access your device's GPS location to find nearby restaurants. This data is used only to calculate distances and is not stored on our servers.
- **Recently Viewed:** The App maintains a local history of the last 10 restaurants you viewed for your convenience.
- **Device Information:** For license validation, we may collect device identifiers, operating system version, and app version.

### Information NOT Collected

- We do **NOT** collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or payment information through the App.
- We do **NOT** track your browsing activity outside of the App.
- We do **NOT** sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
- We do **NOT** use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide App Functionality:** Display nearby restaurants, save your preferences, and maintain your favorites list.
2. **Calculate Distances:** Use your location to show how far restaurants are from you.
3. **Improve User Experience:** Remember your recently viewed restaurants and default settings.
4. **Validate Licenses:** If applicable, verify your license key and device for access to premium features.

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

All user preferences, favorites, and recently viewed history are stored **locally on your device** using secure storage mechanisms. This data is:

- Stored only on your device
- Not transmitted to our servers (except license validation)
- Deleted when you uninstall the App or clear App data

### Server Communication

The App may communicate with our servers for:

- **Restaurant Data:** Fetching restaurant information and daily specials
- **License Validation:** Verifying license keys (if applicable)

These communications are encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS protocols.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Location Access

You can control location access through your device settings. Disabling location access will limit the App's ability to show nearby restaurants or calculate distances.

### Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:

1. **Clear History:** Use the "Clear Recently Viewed" option in Settings
2. **Remove Favorites:** Unfavorite individual restaurants
3. **Uninstall App:** All locally stored data is deleted when you uninstall the App

### Opt-Out

You may opt out of location-based features by denying location permission when prompted or by disabling location services in your device settings.
