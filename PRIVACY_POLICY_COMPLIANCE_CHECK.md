# Privacy Policy Compliance Analysis

## Current Status: ⚠️ NEEDS UPDATES FOR FULL COMPLIANCE

The current privacy policy needs several updates to be fully compliant with major privacy regulations.

---

## GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - EU

### ❌ Missing Required Elements:
1. **Legal Basis for Processing** - Need explicit legal basis (legitimate interest, consent, etc.)
2. **Data Subject Rights** - Missing detailed explanation of GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection)
3. **Data Protection Officer** - No mention of DPO contact information
4. **International Data Transfers** - Need explicit mention of data transfers from EU to US
5. **Data Breach Notification** - Missing breach notification procedures
6. **Cookie Policy** - No mention of cookies or tracking technologies

### ✅ Compliant Elements:
- Clear data retention periods
- Purpose limitation
- Data minimization principles

---

## CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - California

### ❌ Missing Required Elements:
1. **"Do Not Sell" Mechanism** - Missing opt-out link/button
2. **Financial Incentives** - No mention of financial incentives program
3. **Authorized Agents** - Missing process for authorized agents to act on behalf of consumers
4. **Non-Discrimination** - Missing non-discrimination clause
5. **Specific Data Categories** - Need more detailed categorization of personal information
6. **Business/Commercial Purposes** - Missing explicit statement of business purposes

### ✅ Compliant Elements:
- Right to delete
- Right to know
- No sale of personal data (currently)

---

## PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act) - Canada

### ❌ Missing Required Elements:
1. **Consent** - Need explicit consent mechanisms
2. **Purpose Specification** - Need more specific purposes for collection
3. **Use, Disclosure, and Retention Limits** - Need detailed retention policies
4. **Safeguards** - Need specific security measures description
5. **Individual Access** - Need detailed access request procedures
6. **Challenges and Compliance** - Missing complaint procedures

### ✅ Compliant Elements:
- Accountability principle
- Openness principle

---

## CalOPPA (California Online Privacy Protection Act) - California

### ❌ Missing Required Elements:
1. **"How Respond to Do Not Track Signals"** - Missing DNT policy
2. **Third-Party Disclosures** - Need more detailed third-party disclosure practices
3. **Process for Reviewing Changes** - Missing review process description
4. **Effective Date** - Present but needs more prominence
5. **Contact Information** - Present but needs more detail

### ✅ Compliant Elements:
- Privacy policy posted and accessible
- Clear description of data practices

## Required Updates for Full Compliance

### 1. Add Legal Basis (GDPR)
```text
Legal Basis for Processing:
We process your data based on the following legal bases:
- Legitimate Interest: Providing restaurant recommendations
- Consent: When you voluntarily provide preferences
- Contractual Necessity: App functionality
```

### 2. Add Data Subject Rights (GDPR)
```text
Your Rights Under GDPR:
- Right to Access: Request copies of your data
- Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
- Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
- Right to Portability: Request data in machine-readable format
- Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
- Right to Restrict: Limit processing of your data
```

### 3. Add CCPA Compliance Elements
```text
California Consumer Rights:
- Right to Know: What personal information we collect
- Right to Delete: Delete personal information we collect
- Right to Opt-Out: Sale of personal information (we do not sell)
- Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate for exercising privacy rights

Do Not Sell My Personal Information: [Link/Button]
```

### 4. Add International Transfer Notice (GDPR)
```text
International Data Transfers:
Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. 
We ensure appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses.
```

### 5. Add Data Breach Procedures (GDPR)
```text
Data Breach Notification:
In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected individuals and authorities within 72 hours when required by law.
```

### 6. Add Cookie/Tracking Policy (All Regulations)
```text
Cookies and Tracking:
We do not use cookies for tracking. The app may use analytics that do not collect personal information.
```

### 7. Add Detailed Consent Mechanisms (PIPEDA)
```text
Consent:
By using our app, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.
You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting the app or clearing app data.
```
