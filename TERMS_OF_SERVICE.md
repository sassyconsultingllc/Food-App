## 2. Description of the Service

Foodie Finder is a free mobile application that helps users discover nearby restaurants. It offers:

- A spinner/randomizer for picking where to eat
- Multi-source restaurant search using Google Places, Foursquare, HERE Maps, and OpenStreetMap
- Favorite restaurants (stored on your device only)
- Private personal notes on favorited restaurants
- Anonymous public "Community Tips" about restaurants
- Menu photo viewing and optional user-uploaded menu photos

- Culver's Flavor of the Day for US users
- Natural-language ("AI") restaurant search

The App does not require an account. There is no sign-in.
