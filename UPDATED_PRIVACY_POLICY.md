# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 28, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 28, 2026

## Introduction

Foodie Finder is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Default Preferences:** ZIP code and search radius for restaurant recommendations
- **Favorites:** Restaurants you mark as favorites
- **Personal Notes:** Optional notes you add to restaurants

### Information Collected Automatically
- **App Usage:** Recently viewed restaurants for quick access
- **Device Information:** App version and device type for compatibility

### Information NOT Collected
- We do not collect personal identifiers like name, email, or phone
- We do not track your location continuously
- We do not collect search history or browsing behavior

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide personalized restaurant recommendations
- Save your preferences and favorites
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Display recently viewed restaurants for convenience

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
All user preferences, favorites, and recently viewed history are stored **locally on your device** using secure storage mechanisms. This data is:

- Stored only on your device
- Not transmitted to our servers
- Deleted when you uninstall the App or clear App data

### Cloudflare Database Cache
Restaurant data from Google Places API is cached in **Cloudflare D1** database to:

- Improve app performance and loading times
- Optimize Google API costs (we pay for this data)
- Reduce redundant API calls to Google services
- Provide consistent restaurant information across all users

**Important:** Only restaurant data from our paid Google Places API is stored in Cloudflare. No personal user data, preferences, search history, or location tracking is stored in the cloud.

### Data Retention
- Restaurant cache data: Automatically refreshed every 30 days
- Local user data: Remains on your device until you delete it
- You can clear all local data through Settings > Data Management > Clear History

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
- **Protection of Rights:** To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified)

## Third-Party Services

The App may display information from third-party sources including:
- Restaurant data from Google Places API (paid service)
- Culver's Flavor of the Day information (via official API)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## Cloudflare Infrastructure & Google API
We use Cloudflare's global infrastructure to:
- Cache Google Places API data for faster access
- Optimize costs for our paid Google API usage
- Provide reliable service worldwide
- Reduce environmental impact through efficient edge computing

**Google Places API:** We pay Google for restaurant data access and cache responses to provide the best user experience while managing API costs.

Cloudflare processes data according to their privacy policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

## Your Rights and Choices

### Location Access
- The app requests location access only when you use GPS features
- You can deny location access and manually enter ZIP codes
- Location data is not stored or transmitted to our servers

### Data Deletion
- Clear your history: Settings > Data Management > Clear History
- Remove favorites: Long-press any restaurant in your favorites list
- Delete all data: Uninstall the app or clear app data in device settings

### Opt-Out
- Disable location access in device settings
- Use manual ZIP code entry instead of GPS
- Clear local data regularly through app settings

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- Local data is encrypted using device security features
- Cloudflare provides enterprise-grade security for cached restaurant data
- Regular security updates and monitoring

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Updating the effective date above
- Sending in-app notifications for significant changes
