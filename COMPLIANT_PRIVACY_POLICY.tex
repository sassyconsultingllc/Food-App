\section{Introduction}

Foodie Finder is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA, and CalOPPA.

\section{Information We Collect}

\subsection{Information You Provide}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Default Preferences:} ZIP code and search radius for restaurant recommendations
    \item \textbf{Favorites:} Restaurants you mark as favorites
    \item \textbf{Personal Notes:} Optional notes you add to restaurants
    \item \textbf{Contact Information:} Only when you voluntarily contact support
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Information Collected Automatically}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{App Usage:} Recently viewed restaurants for quick access
    \item \textbf{Device Information:} App version, device type, and operating system for compatibility
    \item \textbf{Performance Data:} Crash reports and app performance metrics (no personal identifiers)
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Information NOT Collected}
\begin{itemize}
    \item We do not collect personal identifiers like name, email, or phone unless voluntarily provided
    \item We do not track your location continuously (only when you explicitly use GPS features)
    \item We do not collect search history or browsing behavior
    \item We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
\end{itemize}

\section{Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)}

We process your personal data based on the following legal bases:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Legitimate Interest:} Providing personalized restaurant recommendations and improving app performance
    \item \textbf{Consent:} When you voluntarily provide preferences and personal notes
    \item \textbf{Contractual Necessity:} Essential for app functionality and service delivery
    \item \textbf{Legal Obligation:} When required by applicable laws
\end{enumerate}

\section{How We Use Your Information}

We use your information to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Provide personalized restaurant recommendations
    \item Save your preferences and favorites
    \item Improve app performance and user experience
    \item Display recently viewed restaurants for convenience
    \item Respond to customer support requests
    \item Comply with legal obligations
\end{itemize}

\section{Data Storage}

\subsection{Local Storage}
All user preferences, favorites, and recently viewed history are stored \textbf{locally on your device} using secure storage mechanisms. This data is:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Stored only on your device
    \item Not transmitted to our servers
    \item Deleted when you uninstall the App or clear App data
    \item Encrypted using device security features
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Cloudflare Database Cache}
Restaurant data from Google Places API is cached in \textbf{Cloudflare D1} database to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Improve app performance and loading times
    \item Optimize Google API costs (we pay for this data)
    \item Reduce redundant API calls to Google services
    \item Provide consistent restaurant information across all users
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Important:} Only restaurant data from our paid Google Places API is stored in Cloudflare. No personal user data, preferences, search history, or location tracking is stored in the cloud.

\subsection{International Data Transfers (GDPR)}
Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses and adequate protection measures in accordance with GDPR requirements.

\subsection{Data Retention}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Restaurant cache data:} Automatically refreshed every 30 days
    \item \textbf{Local user data:} Remains on your device until you delete it
    \item \textbf{Support communications:} Retained for 2 years for service quality purposes
    \item \textbf{You can clear all local data} through Settings > Data Management > Clear History
\end{itemize}

\section{Your Rights and Choices}

\subsection{GDPR Rights (EU Residents)}
You have the following rights under GDPR:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Right to Access:} Request copies of your personal data
    \item \textbf{Right to Rectification:} Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
    \item \textbf{Right to Erasure:} Request deletion of your personal data
    \item \textbf{Right to Portability:} Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
    \item \textbf{Right to Object:} Object to processing of your personal data
    \item \textbf{Right to Restrict:} Limit how we process your personal data
\end{enumerate}

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@sassyconsultingllc.com. We will respond within 30 days.

\subsection{CCPA Rights (California Residents)}
As a California consumer, you have the right to:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Right to Know:} What personal information we collect, use, and disclose
    \item \textbf{Right to Delete:} Delete personal information we collect and maintain
    \item \textbf{Right to Opt-Out:} Sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
    \item \textbf{Right to Non-Discrimination:} We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Do Not Sell My Personal Information:} We do not sell personal information. If you have questions, contact us at info@sassyconsultingllc.com

\subsection{PIPEDA Rights (Canada)}
Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Know why we collect, use, and disclose your personal information
    \item Request access to your personal information
    \item Challenge the accuracy of your personal information
    \item Request correction of inaccurate information
    \item Withdraw consent where appropriate
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Location Access}
\begin{itemize}
    \item The app requests location access only when you use GPS features
    \item You can deny location access and manually enter ZIP codes
    \item Location data is not stored or transmitted to our servers
    \item You can revoke location access at any time in device settings
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Data Deletion}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Clear your history: Settings > Data Management > Clear History
    \item Remove favorites: Long-press any restaurant in your favorites list
    \item Delete all data: Uninstall the app or clear app data in device settings
    \item Request deletion: Contact us at info@sassyconsultingllc.com
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Opt-Out Choices}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Disable location access in device settings
    \item Use manual ZIP code entry instead of GPS
    \item Clear local data regularly through app settings
    \item Do Not Track: We respect Do Not Track signals and do not use tracking technologies
\end{itemize}

\section{Data Sharing}

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Legal Requirements:} If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
    \item \textbf{Protection of Rights:} To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
    \item \textbf{Business Transfers:} In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified)
    \item \textbf{Service Providers:} With trusted third-party service providers who process data on our behalf (Cloudflare, Google)
\end{itemize}

\section{Third-Party Services}

The App may display information from third-party sources including:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Restaurant data from Google Places API (paid service)
    \item Culver's Flavor of the Day information (via official API)
\end{itemize}

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

\section{Cloudflare Infrastructure \& Google API}
We use Cloudflare's global infrastructure to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Cache Google Places API data for faster access
    \item Optimize costs for our paid Google API usage
    \item Provide reliable service worldwide
    \item Reduce environmental impact through efficient edge computing
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Google Places API:} We pay Google for restaurant data access and cache responses to provide the best user experience while managing API costs.

Cloudflare processes data according to their privacy policy: \url{https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/}

\section{Cookies and Tracking}

We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies in our mobile app. The app may use analytics services that do not collect personal information. We respect Do Not Track signals.

\section{Data Security}

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Local data is encrypted using device security features
    \item Cloudflare provides enterprise-grade security for cached restaurant data
    \item Regular security updates and monitoring
    \item Access controls and authentication for our systems
    \item Data encryption in transit and at rest where applicable
\end{itemize}

\section{Data Breach Notification}

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Notify affected individuals without undue delay and where feasible within 72 hours
    \item Notify relevant regulatory authorities as required by law
    \item Provide information about the breach and measures taken
\end{itemize}

\section{Children's Privacy}

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

\section{Changes to This Privacy Policy}

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Posting the new policy in the app
    \item Updating the effective date above
    \item Sending in-app notifications for significant changes
    \item Email notification for users who have contacted support
\end{itemize}

\section{Review Process}

We review this privacy policy annually and as needed to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations. Material changes will be communicated to users as described above.

\section{Complaint Procedures}

If you have concerns about our privacy practices, you may:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Contact us directly at info@sassyconsultingllc.com
    \item File a complaint with the relevant privacy authority:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item EU: Your local data protection authority
        \item California: California Attorney General
        \item Canada: Privacy Commissioner of Canada
    \end{itemize}
\end{enumerate}

We will investigate and respond to all complaints promptly.

\section{Financial Incentives (CCPA)}

We do not offer financial incentives for the disclosure of personal information. If we ever offer such a program, we will provide clear notice and obtain your consent.

\section{Authorized Agents (CCPA)}

You may designate an authorized agent to exercise your CCPA rights on your behalf. We will verify the agent's authority before processing requests.

\section{Non-Discrimination (CCPA)}

We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights, including by:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Denying goods or services
    \item Charging different prices or rates
    \item Providing different levels of service
    \item Suggesting you may receive a different price or rate
\end{itemize}

\section{Contact Us}

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, please contact us:

\textbf{Email:} info@sassyconsultingllc.com \\
\textbf{Website:} \url{https://privacy.sassyconsultingllc.com/foodie-finder} \\
\textbf{Privacy Officer:} Available at the above email for GDPR-related inquiries

\section{California Residents}

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA). This policy is designed to comply with these laws.

\section{International Users}

Your data may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country. We take appropriate measures to ensure your data is protected according to this privacy policy regardless of where it is processed, including Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfers.
